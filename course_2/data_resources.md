# Data Resources
Publicly available datasets.

**Set of Maximal Over-all Leverage (SMOL) dataset**: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2502.12301
- 6,100,000 word corpus from Google, featuring professionally translated parallel data in English and 115 underrepresented languages. 
- available for free
- includes translated sentences and documents and 
- can be downloaded from HuggingFace. https://huggingface.co/datasets/google/smol/tree/main

**TICO-19**: https://tico-19.github.io/
- 69,700 word corpus created through a cross-institutional effort involving Carnegie Mellon University, George Mason University, Johns Hopkins University, and several industry partners (Amazon, Appen, Facebook, Google, Microsoft, and Translated) in collaboration with Translations Without Borders. This dataset was developed in response to the 2019 COVID-19 pandemic, containing COVID-19 materials for a variety of languages to support professional translators and train state-of-the-art machine translation models.

**MasakhaNER**: https://aclanthology.org/2022.emnlp-main.298.pdf
- designed for named entity recognition (NER) tasks across 20 African languages. 
- Has between 4,800 and 11,000 sentences per language.
- created by the Masakhane African NLP community 
- Available on GitHub. https://github.com/masakhane-io/masakhane-ner/tree/main/MasakhaNER2.0

**TinyStories**: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2305.07759
- synthetic dataset of over 2 million short stories
- created by Microsoft Research.
- designed for young children
- Uses only words typically understood by 3 to 4 year olds. 
- available on HuggingFace. https://huggingface.co/datasets/roneneldan/TinyStories

**AfriSenti**: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2302.08956
- sentiment analysis dataset aimed at understanding the diverse perspectives of people across the African continent. 
- Includes more than 110,000 annotated tweets in 14 African languages. 
- sourced from X
- anonymizes personal and sensitive information while removing URLs. 
- Download from HuggingFace. https://huggingface.co/datasets/shmuhammad/AfriSenti-twitter-sentiment
